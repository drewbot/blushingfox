A little about me

Blushing Fox Photography has been my dream since I started studying Black & White Photography when I was 15.

I am all about fun, love and self-confidence. I strive to evoke and capture these feelings so that my clients have images to reflect how good they can feel and look. I want you to look back at your album and remember how amazing you felt that day, whether it is because you were marrying your best friend or because you had a day spent getting pampered with professional hair and makeup and an awesome, fun photo shoot!

Blushing Beauties and Foxy Ladies

While we are all a little bit of both of these categories, let me explain what they are about. Blushing Beauties are taking a step in a new direction and excited or "Blushing" about their futures. They are the Engaged Couples, Brides and Mothers-to-be. Foxy Ladies are women who want pictures looking Foxy, whether for themselves or for a significant other. They are the Pinup Girls, Boudoir Babes, and Lifestyle Portraits. Whatever your style, you will have a fun and empowering photo session!

Give me a ring or drop me a line and let me help you be a Blushing Fox!
